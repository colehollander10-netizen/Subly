Finn - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

What Finn does with your data

Finn helps you manually track free trials and subscriptions so you can get reminded before a charge happens. You can type details yourself or paste trial-related text for on-device parsing. Finn does not connect to your Gmail, email inbox, bank, card, or any financial account.

What we collect

  • Trial and subscription details you enter, such as service name, charge date, amount, billing cycle, status, and notification preference
  • Trial details extracted on-device from text you choose to paste into the app
  • Basic app preferences, such as onboarding state and preview-data settings

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not connect to, read, or store your email inbox
  • We do not connect to your bank, card, or financial accounts
  • We do not transmit pasted text or parsed trial details to any server
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way

Where your data lives

All app data is stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your phone. There is no Finn server, database, or backend for your trial or subscription data.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Finn schedules local notifications on your device for upcoming trial or subscription charge dates. Notification scheduling does not require a server.

Your rights

You can edit or delete tracked trials and subscriptions inside the app. Deleting the app removes locally stored Finn data from your device.
